Ductable AC

Ductable air conditioners, also known as ducted air conditioners, are a type of cooling system that is designed to cool an entire space through a network of ducts. These systems are commonly used in larger buildings, such as office buildings, malls, and hotels, where cooling individual rooms with separate units would be inefficient. Ductable ACs are often more powerful than traditional split AC units, across a larger area.

Know more

Window AC

Window air conditioners, also known as room air conditioners, are an excellent cooling solution for single rooms or small apartments. These units are installed in a window or through a specially designed opening in a wall. They are compact and typically more affordable than central air conditioning systems. Window ACs are available in various sizes to suit different room dimensions, providing efficient cooling and dehumidification.

Know more

HVAC Consulting

HVAC consulting services provide expert guidance and support for heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. These services typically include system design, energy efficiency analysis, equipment selection, and project management. HVAC consultants work closely with clients to understand their needs and goals, ensuring that the proposed solutions meet both regulatory requirements and budget constraints.

Know more

Split AC

"Split air conditioners, often referred to simply as split ACs, are popular cooling solutions for homes and offices. They consist of two main units: an indoor unit that contains the cooling coil and an outdoor unit that houses the compressor and condenser. The indoor unit is typically sleek and compact, making it easy to install in various settings.

Know more

VRV/VRF Maintinance System

A VRV/VRF maintenance system is a crucial aspect of ensuring the optimal performance and longevity of Variable Refrigerant Volume/Flow air conditioning systems. These systems are highly efficient and versatile, offering individual control over multiple indoor units, making them ideal for commercial and residential buildings.

Geyser

A geyser is a water heating appliance that uses a gas or electric heating element to heat water for domestic or commercial use. Geysers are commonly used in homes, hotels, and other establishments to provide hot water for bathing, washing, and other purposes.

Know more

Fridge

A fridge, short for refrigerator, is an essential appliance found in most homes and businesses. It is designed to keep food and drinks cold, thereby slowing down the growth of bacteria and prolonging the freshness of perishable items. Modern fridges come in various sizes and styles, from compact mini-fridges to large French door models with advanced features like ice makers and water dispensers.
